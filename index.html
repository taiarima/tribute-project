Fan Deng

Champion of Learning and Literacy

A Brief Biography

Fan Deng was born to a middle class family in Xi'an, China on March 24, 1976. Although his parents were both teachers, he grew up as a typical student, not being particularly passionate towards education. As a student, he said that he hated exams, and some of his teachers remarked that his grades were good, but not outstanding. Indeed, Fan Deng celebrated after graduating from his master's degree program by throwing away all of his textbooks and swearing to his friends that he would never study again.

After gaining attention during his college years as a skillful member of his university's debate team, Fan Deng began a career working as a television host. He began his career with high levels of confidence, but quickly discovered that none of his television programs gained traction. After trying everything he could think of to resolve the situation, he felt like his back was against a wall. Finally, he turned to books in search of a solution.

One of the first books which Fan Deng focused on was "7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen Covey.
